About me

I am a doctoral candidate in Operations Research at the Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania, advised by Prof. Gad Allon. My primary research interests are in the design and analysis of data-driven and human-centric solutions to problems in operations management. I earned my ScB in Computer Engineering and Applied Mathematics-Economics from Brown University and my SM in Computation for Design and Optimization at MIT, where I worked with Prof. Georgia Perakis and Prof. Y. Karen Zheng. Prior to Wharton, I worked as a software engineer at Oracle, a quantitative strategist at Goldman Sachs, and a data scientist at Deloitte Consulting.
